Heading somewhere worth sharing?
With Piner you get to share your best moments per location allowing friends and followers know what you've been up to whether they are on or off that location. Piner will notify a friend when they are in an area with your trail, and guide them through your pins. You can add notes, pictures and videos to your trail.

360 degrees video

Sharing 360 degrees videos expands the experience for friends and followers on your trail. These videos are recorded with attached 360 cameras. When on location, moving the device around in playback (or panning in all directions when off location) allows those following your trial relive your exact moments.
