everything. around you

Look around, everyone has something to offer ;)
Square converts your neighbourhood into a resourceful space. Designed to encourage peer to peer exchange, Square uses augmented reality to capture and display live interests on map and on camera.
You may add multiple interests, tag values to each interest, and switch availability.

Find on the go

Square allows you to find while you move and alerts you when searched interests are within range. You can share searches and compare results with friends and groups.
Improve search settings for preferred range and specific features.

Customise your avatars

Similar to using objects as pins, Square also allows you to create animated personalised avatars. You can spin around, wink an eye, laugh out loud, make a polka face or use a combination of gestures when sharing a location or interest, or making a new friend.
